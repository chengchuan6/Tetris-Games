About Tetris Games

The Legacy of Tetris

Tetris, created by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984, revolutionized the gaming world with its simple yet addictive gameplay. The concept of arranging falling tetrominoes (geometric shapes made up of four squares) to create complete lines has become one of the most recognized game mechanics globally.

Today, this classic puzzle game continues to evolve with new variations, mechanics, and visual styles. Our collection brings together the best modern interpretations of this timeless concept, from classic experiences to innovative new takes.

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • ► Left/Right Arrows: Move the falling piece horizontally
  • ► Down Arrow: Accelerate the piece's descent
  • ► Up Arrow: Rotate the piece clockwise
  • ► Space: Hard drop (instantly places the piece at the bottom)

Tetris Strategy Tips

  • ► Always leave space for the I-piece (the long straight tetromino) to clear four lines at once
  • ► Try to keep the stack as flat as possible to give yourself more options
  • ► Learn to use the "wall kick" technique to rotate pieces in tight spots
  • ► Play at a comfortable speed - accuracy is often more important than speed
